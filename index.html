I am currently a pre-doctoral fellow at the Flatiorn Institute Center for Computational Astrophysics, and a graduate student in astronomy at the University of Maryland.

My research interests include compact objects, gravitational dynamics, high-order numerical methods, plasma physics, stellar evolution, Bayesian inference, and high-performance computing.

Prior to attending the University of Maryland, I studied physics and astronomy at the University of Illinois.
